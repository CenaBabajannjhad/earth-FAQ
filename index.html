what is earth ?

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. Almost all of Earth's water is contained in its global ocean, covering 70.8% of Earth's crust. where is earth ?

Earth Earth is the planet we live on, one of eight planets in our solar system and the only known place in the universe to support life. Earth is the third planet from the sun, after Mercury and Venus, and before Mars. It is about 150 million kilometers (about 93 million miles) from the sun. moon ?

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. It orbits at an average distance of 384,400 km (238,900 mi), about 30 times the diameter of Earth. Tidal forces between Earth and the Moon have over time synchronized the Moon's orbital period (lunar month) with its rotation period (lunar day) at 29.5 Earth days, causing the same side of the Moon to always face Earth. The Moon's gravitational pull and to a lesser extent, the Sun's are the main drivers of Earth's tides.
